Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our AI solutions and services

How long does it take to build an AI chatbot?

Our AI chatbot development typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. Simple chatbots can be ready in 2 weeks, while advanced conversational AI with custom integrations may take 4-6 weeks. We provide regular updates throughout the development process.

What is the ROI on workflow automation?

Our clients typically see a 150-300% ROI within the first 6 months. Workflow automation reduces manual errors by 90%, cuts processing time by 60-80%, and frees up staff for higher-value tasks. Most businesses recover their investment within 3-4 months.

Do you offer ongoing support for your solutions?

Yes, we provide comprehensive support packages including 24/7 monitoring, regular updates, performance optimization, and technical support. Our AI systems continuously learn and improve, and we ensure they stay current with the latest technologies and your evolving business needs.

Can you integrate AI with our existing systems?

Absolutely! We specialize in seamless integration with existing systems. Our AI solutions can connect with your CRM, ERP, databases, APIs, and third-party applications. We ensure minimal disruption to your current operations while maximizing the value of your existing infrastructure.

What makes Nabih AI different from other AI agencies?

We're not just an AI agency; we're building an ecosystem of innovative solutions. Our unique approach combines cutting-edge AI with deep industry knowledge, local market expertise in Egypt, and a proven track record of delivering measurable business impact.
